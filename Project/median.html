The Median is the "middle" of a sorted list of numbers.

To find the Median, sort the numbers from smallest to largest and find the middle number.

When you have an even number of numbers, there will be 2 numbers in the middle. In this case, locate those 2 numbers, and find the mean of them.

The mean of the 2 numbers in the middle will be your median
