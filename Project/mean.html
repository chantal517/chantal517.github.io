First, add up all the individual numbers in the sequence to get the sum

Next divide that sum by how many numbers there are in total.

Calculate the mean of these numbers : 6, 11, 7

Add the numbers: 6 + 11 + 7 = 24

Divide by how many numbers (there are 3 numbers): 24 / 3 = 8

The mean is 8

Calculate the mean of these numbers : 9, 10, 4, 8, 4

Add the numbers: 9 + 10 + 4 + 8 + 4 = 35

Divide by how many numbers (there are 5 numbers): 35 / 5 = 7

The mean is 7
