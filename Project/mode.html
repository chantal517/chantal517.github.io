The Mode is the number that occurs most often in the list of numbers.

To find the mode, sort the numbers from smallest to largest and count the number of times each number occurs.

The number(s) that occur the most frequently is your mode.

Calculate the mode of these numbers : 11, 6, 7, 6

Sort the numbers : 6, 6, 7, 11

Keep a count of each number 6 - 2 times, 7 - 1 time, 11 - 1 time

6 occurs the most frequently

The mode is 6

Calculate the mode of these numbers : 2, 8, 2, 4, 6, 4

Sort the numbers: 2, 2, 4, 4, 6, 8

Keep a count of each number 2 - 2 times, 4 - 2 times, 6 - 1 time, 8 - 1 time

4 and 2 both occur twice. So they are both the mode.

The mode is 4 and 2
